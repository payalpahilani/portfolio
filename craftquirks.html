Let's Work Together

Project : Craftquirks

September 2019 - Feb 2020

Craftquirks, my final year college project, is a unique e-commerce platform dedicated to offering a carefully curated selection of handmade cards and gifts. Designed with the intent to add a personal touch to presents for friends and family, this project allowed me to blend my passion for web development with my appreciation for artisanal craftsmanship.

My Contribution

Full-Stack Development:

Managed all aspects of frontend and backend development to ensure a seamless and user-friendly shopping experience.

Payment Gateway Integration:

Implemented Paykun as the payment gateway, providing users with a secure and hassle-free checkout process.

User Experience Design:

Focused on creating a design that reflects the unique nature of the products and enhances the overall shopping experience

Project Management:

Oversaw the project from inception to deployment, ensuring timely completion and quality output.

Technologies Used

Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript – For crafting a visually appealing and intuitive user interface.

Backend: PHP, MySQL, ASP.NET – For implementing functionalities such as user authentication, product management, and secure payment processing.

Payment Gateway: Integrated Paykun for secure and seamless payment transactions.

Others: Git, GitHub – For version control and project management.
