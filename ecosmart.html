Project : Ecosmart

January 2024 - Febuary 2024

Design Mock up

As a collaborator and contributor to the EcoSmart project, my role was pivotal in designing the mobile interface for this innovative home automation ecosystem. EcoSmart was born out of a desire to create a smarter and more sustainable living environment, and our team was committed to transforming this vision into reality.

My Contribution:

Mobile Design:

I took the lead in designing the mobile app interface for EcoSmart. My focus was on creating an intuitive and user-friendly experience that would enable users to seamlessly control and monitor their home's energy consumption. The design process involved extensive wireframing, user journey mapping, and prototyping to ensure that the app was not only visually appealing but also highly functional.

Collaborative Design Process:

Working closely with the team, I participated in thorough wireframing sessions where we mapped out the user interactions across various touchpoints. This collaborative approach was crucial in ensuring a cohesive and integrated user experience across the entire EcoSmart ecosystem.

Visual Identity:

I contributed to the development of a comprehensive style guide that defined the color palette, typography, and design elements for the mobile app. This style guide served as a blueprint for maintaining consistency and unity in our design language throughout the development process.
