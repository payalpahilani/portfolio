Let's Work Together

Project : WonderWorld

Febuary 2024 - March 2024

VR Journey Through the Seven Wonders of the World.

As part of a collaborative team, I embarked on an ambitious project to create an immersive virtual reality experience showcasing the Seven Wonders of the World. Our goal was to combine our passion for VR technology with our fascination for historical landmarks, offering users an unparalleled journey through time and space.

My Contribution:

Design and Planning:

I played a key role in the initial planning and design phase, utilizing Figma to create detailed wireframes and high-fidelity mockups. My focus was on designing an intuitive user interface and interactive components that would enhance the immersive experience.

Style Guide Development:

I contributed to the development of a comprehensive style guide, ensuring consistency in visual elements and creating a cohesive aesthetic throughout the VR experience.

Unity Integration:

Transitioning from design to development, I was involved in integrating our Figma components into Unity. This process was crucial in transforming our static designs into a dynamic, fully functional VR environment.

3D Asset Integration:

Leveraging Unity's capabilities, I assisted in incorporating 3D assets to meticulously recreate each of the Seven Wonders. This involved close attention to detail to ensure accuracy and realism in the virtual representations.
